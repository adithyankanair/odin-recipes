Chicken Biriyani

Malabar Chicken Biryani (also known as Thalassery Biryani) is a fragrant, aromatic rice dish from Kerala. It features lightly spiced chicken masala layered with thin, short-grain Khaima or Jeerakasala rice cooked in ghee, and topped with golden fried onions, cashews, and raisins

Ingredients

  • Rice (khaima) - 4 cups
  • Chicken pieces - 1kg
  • Hot water - 7 cups
  • Ghee - 3 Table spoon
  • Onion - 4nos(medium)
  • Ginger - 1 inch piece
  • Garlic - 8 cloves
  • Green chilli -4 nos
  • Tomato - 2 nos
  • Yogurt -1/2 cup
  • Cashew nut -15 nos
  • Raisins - 15 nos
  • Chilli Powder - 1/2 table spoon
  • Coriander Powder - 3 table spoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1 pinch
  • Garam Masala - 1/2 tea spoon
  • Cinnamon - 3 piece
  • Clove - 10 nos
  • Cardamom - 5 nos
  • Pepper - 10 nos
  • Coriander leaves - 4 sprigs
  • Mint/pudina leaves - 4 sprigs
  • Pineapple - 1/2 cup
  • Coconut oil - 3 Table spoon
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash biriyani rice , soak in water for 30 minutes and drain it well
  2. Grind ginger, garlic and green chilli into a paste. Add curd and salt to this paste.Marinate chicken pieces with this paster for minimum 30 minutes.
  3. Now prepare the garnishes for chicken biriyani.Heat 1 table spoon of ghee in a cooking vessel, fry cashew nuts, raisins and 1 finely sliced onion separately til goldern brown and keep aside.(while frying onions, when it is almost done add 1 tea spoon of sugar so that the fired onions remain crisp throughout
  4. Heat 3 table spoon coconut oil in a vessel, put remaining 3 finely sliced onions and salt to taste. Saute until golden brown. Reduce the flame, add chilli, coriander, turmeric and garam masala powders. Stir it for 2 minutes. Add sliced tomatoes and saute it for few minutes.
  5. To this add marinated chicken pieces and stir occasionally for 5 minutes. Add 1 cup of water and cook it covered till done (reduce the flame to medium when it starts boiling)
  6. Heat 1 table spoon of ghee in another cooking vessel, put cinnamon, cloves, cardamom & pepper and stir it. Put rice and stir for 2 minutes. Pour 7 cups of hot water and salt as needed. Cook it covered till the water is absorbed by the rice completely. (Reduce the flame when it starts boiling. Take care not to over cook the rice.)
  7. Spread evenly 1 table spoon of ghee in a vessel and layer it with rice and cooked chicken. Arrange 5 layers with rice and chicken. The bottom layer and the top layer should be rice. Garnish each layer with fried onion, cashews, raisins, pineapple, finely chopped coriander and mint (pudina) leaves. Pour 1 table spoon of Ghee on the top of Chicken Biryani. Cover the vessel.
  8. Heat a heavy tawa, put the biryani vessel on the tawa and heat it in a very low flame for 15 minutes. Turn off the flame and let it unopened for another 10 minutes. (Instead of cooking it on a heavy tawa, you can also cook it in a pre-heated oven.)
  9. Serve chicken biryani hot with salad, pappad, lime pickle and lemon tea.
